Re-Cyclical Energy: Flywheel Energy Storage

Our grid scale or home use energy storage technology combines the reliability of flywheel energy storage with the affordability and environmentally friendly choice of using recycled polymers and glass.

Materials

Glass fiber resin composites have been the materials of choice for some time for high performance flywheel technology, only being beat out by carbon fiber but at much higher price point. We take a similiar approach of materials but instead use recycled glass aggragate and thermoplastic waste to produce a composite that is significantly cheaper and more environmentally friendly.

Reliability

Flywheel energy storage is some of the most reliable long term energy solutions, boasting incredible lifespans, cycle durations, with quick discharge and charge, and virtually no maintainence. In order to push this even further and increase energy density our energy storage uses magnetic bearing and vaccuum sealing to minimize friction and extend the working life.

Impact

Energy storage is quickly becoming an essential component in the race to renewable dominance, but batteries and pumped hydro come with their own challenges. Flywheels are a great alternative but costs associated with high performance devices have held the technology back, this is where our model comes in.

Cost

As mentioned before due to the choice of using recycled materials our costs are much lower than comparable high performance glass fiber resin composite flywheels despite being fundamentally the same technology. This lower cost makes it easier to scale our solutions and make home use finally within reach for owners of renewable technology that would otherwise turn away from flywheel energy storage due to high costs.
